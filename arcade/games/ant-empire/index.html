🐜 ANT EMPIRE

A zero-player colony simulation built on real stigmergy. Ants leave pheromone trails; trails become roads; roads become empires. Mostly, you just watch.

Nudge the world with the toolbar: drop 🍎 food, place 🪨 rocks, summon 💧 rain (it washes the trails away — watch them rebuild), tap 🔍 then any ant to follow it, and accelerate time up to 16×.
