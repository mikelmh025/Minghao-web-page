🌿 TERRARIUM

A self-running world. Plants sprout and seed, bibbits graze, snappers hunt — and every creature carries heritable traits that drift and adapt across generations. Watch scarce winters breed smaller, thriftier bibbits; watch fast predators breed fast prey.
